Histopathology Workflow — Case Western & Emory / Georgia Tech

Overview

As part of doctoral research at Emory University / Georgia Tech in collaboration with Case Western Reserve University, developed PhenoTIL — a computational pathology pipeline that uses H&E-stained whole slide images to identify immune cell niches and quantify tumor-infiltrating lymphocyte (TIL) spatial patterns predictive of immunotherapy outcomes in lung cancer.

Scope
  • Whole slide image (WSI) ingestion and tissue segmentation
  • Nuclear detection and TIL classification on H&E stains
  • Spatial immune niche quantification and feature engineering
  • Multi-institutional cohort validation across NSCLC and SCLC datasets
  • Published in NPJ Precision Oncology (2023, cited 39+) and subsequent follow-up (2026)
